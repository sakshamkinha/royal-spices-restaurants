Our Story

A Legacy Crafted
with Passion & Spice

Founded in 2008 by Chef Arjun Mehta, Royal Spice began as a humble dream — to bring the depth of India's culinary heritage to Gurgaon's cosmopolitan table. Today, we serve over 200 hand-curated dishes spanning three great cuisines, all prepared in an open kitchen where every flame tells its own tale.

Our philosophy is simple: the finest ingredients, the oldest recipes, and the warmth of genuine hospitality. Whether you arrive for a quiet dinner for two or a grand celebration, you leave as family.
